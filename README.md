# Student Mental Health Analysis using SQL

## About this project
I worked on this small SQL project to understand how the duration of stay affects students’ mental health.

The idea was to see whether students who stay for a shorter time experience more stress compared to those who stay longer.

## What I did
- Created a dataset with student stay duration and mental health scores  
- Used SQL to group students based on how long they stayed  
- Calculated average depression, anxiety, and social connectedness scores  
- Compared the results across different groups  

## What I found
- Students who stayed for a shorter time had higher depression and anxiety  
- Students who stayed longer had better social connection  
- It looks like people slowly adjust over time and feel more comfortable  

## Final thought
This was a simple project, but it helped me understand how to use SQL for real analysis and how to turn data into meaningful insights.
